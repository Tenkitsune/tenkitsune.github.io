Welcome



Thank you very much for taking the time to visit our website! We are excited to have the opportunity to share this wonderful life event with our friends and family. Please feel free to read about our story and all about the events for our big day. You can also read about some other information you can use to help you plan for your visit with us on this day.
During our journey on finding the perfect venue, we decided that the Ozarks would be the perfect place with its beautiful scenery and homey feel. And so, our celebration will be taking place at the beautifully scenic Big Cedar Lodge in Ridgedale, Missouri on November 3rd, 2018. Please know that this is a resort-style location, so in additional to the chapel and reception hall, there will be additional services and accommodation options available on-site. We do hope that you choose to join us in this joyful celebration!
For your reference, the resort is about 15 minutes south of Branson, MO, or about 3 hours and 40 minutes southwest of St. Louis.

About RSVP

You will still have the option to RSVP through regular post mail, so feel free to respond in whichever way you choose!
